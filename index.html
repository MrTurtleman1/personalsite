Gameplay

At each unlock you get an emotoken and at the second you get an amulet that lets you use more than one token at once, and challenges to get one more of each token.

Angry

Gets fire powers. He can create a burst of fire around that melts stuff and burns stuff.

Sad

Gets water powers. He can shoot tears from his eyes that act like projectiles.

Scared

Gets rock powers. He can scream. The scream pushes objects, even big ones, and it can break small objects.

Happy

Gets wind powers. He can double jump.
